Session 8 — The Road to Whitethorn

Alive, but the magic is gone.

Perrin came home — alive against a healer's every instinct, his magic stripped away, the family's Cloak of the Archmage crumbled to dust. He retired from adventuring at breakfast. Then a letter changed everything: the Duke of Whitethorn wants his seventh child, Lysander, home to investigate. Three weeks south, the party found Lyre's city with its vineyards blighted, its witches blamed, propaganda in the streets — and a Witches Market where the party gathered rumors and adopted an alarming number of animals.
