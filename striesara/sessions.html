Session 9 — Gingerbread People

Arc: Whitethorn Investigation · Present: Marva Ironfist, Ellowen Moonthorn, Lysander Cross, Taliesin Briarmane, Raeve Nyaxia · Locations: Whitethorn, Witches Market, The Sweet Cart · NPCs: Mama Sugah, The Duke of Whitethorn, Norman

Quick Summary

While Lysander reunites with his father the Duke at the keep and offers Toil & Trouble's help with the blight, the rest of the party lingers at Mama Sugah's sweet cart - where the baked goods turn out to be more than they seem. The whole group falls unnaturally asleep and wakes transformed into gingerbread people, under attack from Mama Sugah's pastry army.

Recap

The market wound down around Mama Sugah's cart: Marva hunted for something savory and exotic, got high instead (a first), and Ellowen, Raeve, and Thistle held space - while Ellowen and Raeve both got the distinct impression there was magic in the baked goods. Across the city, Lysander walked into the keep, was met by Norman the servant, and reunited with his father. He briefed the Duke: the blight may connect to what the party has already seen. Chief Civil Administrator Aldrin Hawthorn has found little, several adventuring parties have given up before even starting, and tension is rising in the market. Lyre offered Toil & Trouble's services. Then everyone got sleepy - even the elf, which is not how that works. The party woke in the dark: cloying smell, rigid bodies, dry mouths. They are gingerbread people on a cookie sheet. Mama Sugah has done something to them, and her pastry army attacked. Battle underway - we ended mid-fight.

Key Events

  • The party gathers at Mama Sugah's Sweet Cart as the market closes; a crowd grows by the fire
  • Ellowen and Raeve both get the impression that magic is in the baked goods
  • Marva gets high for the first time; Ellowen, Raeve, and Thistle hold space for her
  • Correction to earlier records: Lysander is from Whitethorn, not Davin
  • Lysander arrives at the keep, is received by Norman (servant), and is led to his father's study; a warm reunion - his mother worries
  • Lysander briefs the Duke of Whitethorn on the party's recent history; the odd happenings may be related to the blight
  • Chief Civil Administrator Aldrin Hawthorn has not found much; several adventuring parties have given up before even starting
  • Lysander offers Toil & Trouble's assistance as an adventuring party; the Duke mentions tension in the market
  • Family details: Brennan is the knight of an unnamed banner; the Duke would not worry about his sister protesting witches - she is 'not ready for all that'; Loriel is 34; the Duke married young out of obligation; Lyre has been gone three years
  • The whole group becomes unnaturally sleepy - even the elf, which is not how that works; Lysander tries to walk back to the keep
  • The party wakes in the dark - thick cloying smell, rigid bodies, dry mouths - transformed into gingerbread people on a cookie sheet
  • Mama Sugah is responsible; her pastry army attacks; battle ensues (session ends mid-combat)

Session 8 — The Road to Whitethorn

Arc: Whitethorn Investigation · Present: Kessia Brightpage, Marva Ironfist, Ellowen Moonthorn, Lysander Cross, Taliesin Briarmane, Raeve Nyaxia, Varyn Starwhisper · Locations: Davin, Whitethorn, Witches Market · NPCs: Perrin Slatevein, Tryvenis Slatevein, Mirabelle Slatevein, Amber Ters, Caspian Cross, Lady LeCroix, Willow, Mama Sugah, Juniper Hollowtail, Iris Mothpot

Quick Summary

The party returns Perrin to his family, where a master healer confirms he is inexplicably alive but stripped of all magic. After a recovery night, a grand breakfast, and goodbyes (Perrin retires from adventuring; Lady LeCroix learns of her husband's death; the Earl takes the evidence), the Duke of Whitethorn summons Lysander by name. Three weeks south, the party arrives in Lyre's home city to find its vineyards failing to an unexplained blight and its beloved witches being blamed.

Recap

Perrin came home alive - but the healer confirmed his magic is gone and the family's Cloak of the Archmage is dust. Lord Slatevein pressed 300g on the party after Lyre refused payment. Lyre briefed the Earl, who secured the mine and took the evidence, then passed along a letter: their father, the Duke of Whitethorn, wants Lysander's investigative touch at home. Three weeks south (dark things in the woods, an abandoned farmhouse), the party reached Whitethorn's Witches Market: vineyards blighted, witches blamed, propaganda spreading, a stall burned. The party shopped, gathered rumors, and adopted an alarming number of animals. Next session opens with Lysander's scene, then the party meets at Mama Sugah's sweet shop.

Key Events

  • Lyre tries to return the coin purse to Tryvenis, who redirects the payment (300g) to Marva - she absolutely takes it
  • Master healer Amber Ters examines Perrin: body 'should not be alive' but is; soul present; no magical presence; recovery unknown; recommends rest
  • Party overnights in the Slatevein guest wing (magical hot baths); Lyre stays at the earl's keep (2am-6am sleep, level of exhaustion)
  • Kessia delivers letters to Marva and Ellowen and a heavy wooden lockbox (50g and invoices) to Thistle; Marva also receives a locked box with 98g and a business ledger
  • Kessia leaves her new owl familiar to stake out Lyre; Varyn redundantly pays a guard 1g to send word when Lyre leaves (the guard snitches)
  • Earl scene: guards sent to the cave; evidence handed over; mining permit officially revoked; letter from Loriel relays the Duke's request for Lyre's help in Whitethorn; Lyre borrows a covered wagon and horses
  • Lady Slatevein announces Perrin has recovered - but has lost his magical abilities
  • Perrin's account: his mark lit up, smoke, waking in the cave, multiple voices chanting, pain 'ripped from every core of his body'; Renvar coached him and told him to bring his favorite cloak; he declines Lyre's sword and retires from adventuring
  • Lyre returns Perigrine's wedding band to Lady LeCroix and tells her of her husband's death
  • Milo's letter: the Ox statue was moved, cracking the pipe - it's now a fountain in Market Square; he turned down a job for 'strong people who don't ask questions'; a note from Lena asks the group to look at the fountain
  • Marva drinks Ellowen's clarity potion, reasons through logistics, sends the cart home with a letter to Lena and 50g; Lady Slatevein agrees to pass the fountain issue to her husband
  • The 300g reward is split 60g each among Marva, Thistle, Ellowen, Raeve, and Varyn (Lysander excluded by his own refusal; Raeve deliberately included)
  • Family reveals on the road: Lysander is the 7th of 8 children of the Duke of Whitethorn (Corin, Caspian, Brennan, Rhys d., Valyn, Elias, Lysander, Loriel); Raeve is estranged noble elven blood (House Vael'Aras of Talavas) and her sister is engaged to Valyn
  • Three weeks' travel; Ellowen brews constantly and questions animals - dark desiccated-hand creatures more active at night; an abandoned farmhouse found empty with food left behind
  • Arrival in Whitethorn: vineyard blight (empty grape clusters), witches blamed, anti-witch propaganda and a burned stall at the Witches Market, missing-person posters, the Duke's edict protecting licensed witches

  • Raeve adopts Pickles the pygmy pig (12g); Ellowen adopts Soot, Potato, and Hazel; Shimmer the crow on hold
  • Ellowen trades recipes with Willow (sunburst chamomile) and the Bubbles and Brews alchemist (Witches Week formula, ready in 3 days)
  • Marva learns at the Merchant Guild Hall that Whitethorn's vineyard underproduction is disrupting trade routes

Session 7 — Blackroot Hollow

Arc: Davin Investigation · Present: Lysander Cross, Taliesin Briarmane, Marva Ironfist, Raeve Nyaxia, Ellowen Moonthorn, Varyn Starwhisper · Locations: Blackroot Hollow · NPCs: Renvar Ilthis, Perrin Slatevein

Quick Summary

The party travels to Blackroot Hollow, fighting through a corrupted forest and into a cave that reeks of rotting fruit and hot iron. Inside they find Renvar's parasite laboratory and Perrin being tortured as Subject 25. After a brutal fight, Renvar casts Time Stop and escapes. The party recovers Perrin (alive but magically incapacitated), seizes the research notes and parasite specimens, finds the drained Cloak of the Archmage, and learns Subject 14 was likely Perigrine LeCroix.

Recap

Using the Earl's maps, the party reached Blackroot Hollow, battling desiccated creatures through a changing forest and into a cave shaped like a gaping mouth. They uncovered Renvar's parasite laboratory and found Perrin tortured as 'Subject 25.' In a hard fight (Thistle and Marva both downed), Renvar cast Time Stop and vanished. The party recovered Perrin alive but magically out of it, crated the research notes and parasites, found the drained Cloak of the Archmage, and learned Subject 14 was likely Perigrine LeCroix. Lyre left a note: 'Mining permit has hereby been revoked.'

  • Party retreats to normal forest for a short rest; Ellowen gives Marva and Thistle potions of wild shape
  • The cave (Blackroot) smells of rotting fruit and hot iron and looks like a gaping mouth
  • Thistle has a vision; the magic users (Lyre, Raeve, Ellowen) rush him until it dissipates
  • Canisters hold augmented parasites (the desiccated hand creatures)
  • Combat: Thistle goes down, Marva goes down; Lyre heals Thistle
  • Perrin found being tortured by Renvar; Renvar casts Time Stop and disappears
  • Perrin recovered alive but magically incapacitated
  • Varyn finds research notes: Subject 14 may be Peregrine (no body); living parasite used as buffer to keep host from tearing apart; Perrin (Subject 25) was to be first test of latest iteration; complaints work was taking too long
  • A drained, gray Cloak of the Archmage is found

  • Lyre leaves a note at the entrance: 'Mining permit has hereby been revoked'

Kessia rejoins the party (revealed to work for the Vykris Ledger). Lyre escorts everyone to the Earl's Manor, where they meet Captain Edrick Havin and are housed in the chapel. The crucial lead came clear: Renvar had bought mining permits from the Earl for Blackroot Hollow, beyond the lake. With a coin purse from Perrin's mother Mirabelle and Lyre's guidance, the party headed for Blackroot Hollow.

Toil & Trouble arrives in wintry Davin, returns Kara Feldron to her family's farm, and settles in at the Griffon and Oak. At the seedy Goblin's Fire they press Gregor and learn that Renvar personally recruited Perrin into the Adventurer's Gambit; Marva is knocked out in a pit fight with Big John. Lyre brought his brother, the Earl Caspian Cross, up to speed.

Session 4 — Investigation and Travel

Arc: Disappearances and Mysteries · Present: Ellowen Moonthorn, Taliesin Briarmane, Kessia Brightpage, Marva Ironfist, Raeve Nyaxia, Varyn Starwhisper, Lysander Cross · Locations: Aurora Vein, Viekryss, Road to Davin · NPCs: Kassian Dewlight, Lady Vassara, Kara Feldron, Milo, Emmeriss, Dara, Barrel Arms

Quick Summary

Toil & Trouble investigates the simultaneous disappearances of Perrin Slatevein and Renvar Ilthis, gathering information at the Aurora Vein. They discover Perrin's expensive Cloak of the Archmage, Emmeriss's marriage to Dara, and begin traveling to Davin with Kara Feldron. During travel, the party is attacked by mysterious tree-spider creatures and discovers a ring belonging to Perigrine LeCroix.

Recap

Following the disappearances of Perrin and Renvar, Toil & Trouble investigated the Aurora Vein and prepared for travel to Davin. At the casino, they learned about Perrin's artifact-level Cloak of the Archmage, his fascination with Lyre, and the immaculate state of Renvar's room. The party also discovered that Emmeriss is married to Dara (an elf baker) and doesn't owe money to the Bloodletters. Traveling with Kara Feldron on Marva's family wagon pulled by Allshanks, they were ambushed by two tree-like spider creatures with finger-like appendages. After defeating them, they found a ring inscribed with Perigrine LeCroix's initials, suggesting a dark fate for Lyre's target.

Key Events

  • Kassian Dewlight in panic over broken Adventurer's Gambit; party told him to announce the playing field is too damaged to continue
  • Renvar Ilthis's room found immaculate; Lady Vassara possibly last person to see him
  • Perrin Slatevein was wearing a Cloak of the Archmage (artifact-level item worth more than the casino)
  • Perrin's mother revealed to be a powerful magic user; his father serious but alright
  • Perrin keeps a journal but didn't bring it to Viekryss; 'won't shut up about Lyre'
  • Emmeriss kicked out of Aurora Vein; burned through the 5g from Thistle; married to Dara (elf baker); does NOT owe Bloodletters money
  • Lyre and Varyn searched warehouse for antiquities/oddities; checking ledgers for Perigrine LeCroix
  • Lyre's demeanor noticeably improved, seems 'more alive'; has pony keg of wine
  • Lyre displayed subtle numbing effect on Ellowen's family emotions; made her more trusting (party didn't notice)
  • Ellowen revealed four children: Aoife (192 yrs, eldest), Varick (sailor at sea), Emmeriss (troubled), Delick (youngest, in Whitethorn)
  • Ellowen expressed feeling overwhelmed, doesn't remember her own goals anymore
  • Cross is the royal family name in Davin; also surname of all bastards; Lyre is one of 8 children
  • Milo appointed 'assistant to the assistant's assistant' at Stonebell Gym; given whistle and soup key
  • Marva offered family wagon and horse (Allshanks) for travel
  • Party ambushed by two tree-like spider creatures with finger-like appendages during travel
  • Ring found on creature corpse inscribed with Perigrine LeCroix and wife's initials (not magical)
