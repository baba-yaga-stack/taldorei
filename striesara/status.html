The night Lysander pledged Toil & Trouble to his father's blight investigation, Mama Sugah — the market's beloved sweets witch — put the whole party into an unnatural sleep and transformed them into gingerbread people on a cookie sheet. Recent Arc — Sessions 6 → 9

Session 6 — Kessia rejoins the party (revealed to work for the Vykris Ledger). Lyre escorts everyone to the Earl's Manor, where they meet Captain Edrick Havin and are housed in the chapel. Continued questioning of Big John and Gregg yields little before a confrontation lands the party in jail. They learn Renvar secured Blackroot Hollow via mining permits from the Earl, receive a coin purse from Perrin's mother Mirabelle, and set out for Blackroot Hollow.
Session 7 — The party travels to Blackroot Hollow, fighting through a corrupted forest and into a cave that reeks of rotting fruit and hot iron. Inside they find Renvar's parasite laboratory and Perrin being tortured as Subject 25. After a brutal fight, Renvar casts Time Stop and escapes. The party recovers Perrin (alive but magically incapacitated), seizes the research notes and parasite specimens, finds the drained Cloak of the Archmage, and learns Subject 14 was likely Perigrine LeCroix.
Session 8 — The party returns Perrin to his family, where a master healer confirms he is inexplicably alive but stripped of all magic. After a recovery night, a grand breakfast, and goodbyes (Perrin retires from adventuring; Lady LeCroix learns of her husband's death; the Earl takes the evidence), the Duke of Whitethorn summons Lysander by name. Three weeks south, the party arrives in Lyre's home city to find its vineyards failing to an unexplained blight and its beloved witches being blamed.
Session 9 — While Lysander reunites with his father the Duke at the keep and pledges Toil & Trouble to the blight investigation, the rest of the party lingers at Mama Sugah's sweet cart — where the treats hide real magic and Marva gets high for the first time. An unnatural sleep takes everyone (even the elf), and the party wakes as gingerbread people on a cookie sheet, under attack from Mama Sugah's pastry army. The session ends mid-battle.
