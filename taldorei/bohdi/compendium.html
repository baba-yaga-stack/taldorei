The Crew

Anastasia "Ana" Ravenswood

Human · Artificer 11

The smart one, which is why it offends me professionally that someone spent 9th-level money rewriting her memory. Dean Damill's Modify Memory is confirmed; what he erased is not. Her home city, Cathmoira, sleeps drowned in the Shattered Teeth. Ana wards our valuables, cuts anchors out of monsters, and once insulted a stranger into a windstorm. Handle with respect; she builds things that hold grudges.

Asset: Irreplaceable

Finnick "Finn" Marigold Mossglow

Gnome · Illrigger 11

A devil-knight with a patron who makes fireplace calls and a mother who ran a blood cult under a tannery. His name was on the cult plinth, scratched out. He left, which tells you the important thing about him. Reliable in a disposal situation (see: the Pip matter, of which there is no matter). The Sun-Saw was a good purchase.

Asset: Complicated Estate

Grai Malin

Half-Orc · Barbarian 11

Our front line and our conscience, in that order. Bears the Curse of the Hells' echoes: 4d10 psychic every morning, by her own choice, rather than owe anyone a Wish. That's the hardest bargain I've ever watched someone take standing up. She also wears a crown nobody can see, acquired in a spider mine, and I have elected to be professionally incurious. Greatsword of Wounding does what it says.

Asset: Do Not Cross

Imdra d'Vadalis

Human · Druid 11

Speaks with summoned creatures, deploys a healing unicorn I have privately named the reverse-hurting horse, and can wildshape into a giant slug, a skill acquired at a slug party, which I attended, and about which the less said the better. Eight days bonded to Zelthrax made her our dragon whisperer. Underestimated by everyone we fight, which I encourage.

Asset: Versatile

Ki Nightwhisper

Aarakocra · Bard 11

A historian of the Cobalt Soul, which means the crew has a cop in it. A librarian cop. I'm handling it. Carries a demilich's gem-crusted skull as an arcane focus and the Quill of Preservation, an artifact rated for the apocalypse. Currently molting, which he blames on a cursed cloak. Ended a haunting by percussion. Watch what he reports to Griselda; assume everything.

Asset: Reports To Someone Else

Bohdi Shadowtwist

Bugbear · Sorcerer 11 · Spook of the Clasp

Author of this archive. Healer, banisher of ghosts, drawer of escape circles, survivor of a marble hand, and the reason half this crew is alive. Recently promoted to the Clasp inner circle. In a relationship with Mama Mara of Jorenn Village. Net worth: 0 gp and one exceptionally fine black cloak. Assessment: flawless. Self-assessments are permitted; I checked the rules I wrote.

Asset: The Asset

Thrack

Dwarf · Companion · +15 lbs and climbing

Ran into us at our door (NOT trapped, NOT "Thracked"), negotiated 2 gold per night not spent in the mansion (a clause I frame and admire) and became family the day we paid his 200gp debt in Emberhold, his hometown. Knows Yug'Voril's slaver routes. Sells session intelligence for waffles. Loyal as gravity.

Asset: Feed Regularly

Geography

Places

Ebonroot & the Shadebarrow

Home

Where I grew up, now the most important dot on the war map. The Remnants sleep under the barrow. I left Ebonroot without meaning to return; the universe has a documented sense of humor.

Westruun

Current Base of Operations

Clasp bar: the Saddled Planes Cow. Fetch's shop: the Underweave Haberdashery. Anchor storage: the Cobalt Reserve. Murder ghosts: handled. A city that keeps giving.

Jorenn Village

Silver Boom Town · Evacuated & Recovering

Frontier town, no Council presence, Shadewatch keeping order. Home of the Shady Tap Room and its proprietor, to whom my file refers you elsewhere. We saved this town by Skywrite. It had better reopen the Tap Room.

Yug'Voril

Underground Metropolis

Mind flayer architecture, reversed days, a caste system, a mithril ley-core, and a goddess collecting protection weekly. Big, orderly, and wrong in the joints. We did good work in its mines and left before it noticed.

Emon

Friendly Port

Gilmore's, Azalea Park, the Laughing Lamia. Where my hand became a hand again. Sentimental rating: high. Everything in Emon costs money and is worth it.

Cloudwatch

Council Seat

Where the masters of their fields meet and hand out impossible errands in order of urgency. Reached by my teleportation circle, a fact I mention whenever the room goes quiet.

The Bastion of the Fading Dawn

Shrine · Status Unknown

One alabaster tower against an infernal waste, warded by one stubborn priest for eighteen years, breached in an afternoon because we visited. Return trip required, with the cavalry this time.

Syngorn

Where It Started

Elven city, excellent library, briefly on fire. We took one book out of it and the world has been rattling like that wardrobe ever since.
