Memorandum: State of Play

From: B. Shadowtwist, Spook · To: The File

The world: Three Abyssal Anchors exist. Two are deployed: Gap Shadow (aligned to the Chained Oblivion) and the Vecna temple. The third we cut out of a cult mutant and locked in the Cobalt Reserve vault in Westruun. Anchors cannot be teleported; they travel on backs, and backs leave trails. Somebody with a hag, a dead Illithiliche, and a smiling three-eyed sponsor wants the planes cracked open.

The job: Reach the Remnants beneath the Shadebarrow at Ebonroot, the crew that can place our anchor where it must go, and may be able to recover Alorah Vasorin's stolen soul. We hold Buddleia Austan's cursed signet ring, one of the keys to the barrow's iron gate.

The complication: Blaine Kraverrogg is en route to excavate the same barrow and almost certainly holds the second ring. This is now a race, and the finish line is my childhood.

Personal Addendum

Not for distribution

I left Ebonroot a nobody with borrowed magic and a talent for not being noticed. I go back wearing the inner circle's cloak, with five friends, a dwarf, a paper dragon, and the fate of several planes in a bank vault behind me. The barrow under my childhood holds either the way to save the world or one more thing that should have stayed buried. Knowing Ebonroot, it's both.

The official record will say the party went home. The accurate record, this one, will say who opened the gate.
