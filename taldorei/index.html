Session 78 — The Curse of the Hells

Hell always collects.

The murder ghosts died the way everything dies around this party — loudly. Ki killed the ones hiding in the walls by pounding the floor with thunder. Then the real monster surfaced: the Devil Prince's Curse of the Hells, buried in Grai since the Bastion. Only Wish removes it cleanly. Grai took the echoes instead — 4d10 psychic at the end of every long rest, forever. And Camila's second ring is missing: Blaine Kraverrogg likely holds his own key to the barrow.
